Our Team

Meet the passionate individual behind Makuna Mirrors:

As the founder, lead designer, production manager, and customer service representative of Makuna Mirrors, Palak Vyas wears many hats to ensure the success of our company. With a passion for mirror craftsmanship and a commitment to excellence, she leads our team with innovation, creativity, and dedication.

Our Story

Inception and Inspiration

In the bustling heart of the city, Makuna Mirrors was born from a passion for craftsmanship and a love for elegant design. Inspired by the beauty of reflections and the transformative power of mirrors, our journey began with a vision to create mirrors that not only adorned walls but also added depth and character to spaces.

Crafting Excellence

With unwavering dedication and attention to detail, we honed our craft, perfecting the art of mirror-making. From classic designs to contemporary styles, each Makuna Mirror was meticulously crafted by skilled artisans, blending traditional techniques with innovative approaches to create timeless pieces of art.

Reflections of Success

As word of our exquisite mirrors spread, Makuna Mirrors became synonymous with quality, elegance, and sophistication. Our mirrors adorned homes, hotels, and businesses around the world, reflecting the unique personality and style of each space. With every reflection, Makuna Mirrors continues to inspire beauty, creativity, and imagination, weaving its story into the fabric of design history.
